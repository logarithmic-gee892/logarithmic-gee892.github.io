🚀 DVDFab-AI - Improve your video quality with ease

Download DVDFab-AI for Windows

DVDFab-AI uses smart technology to sharpen your videos. This software increases resolution, removes noise, and fixes blurry footage. It works on Windows 10 and Windows 11 computers.

💡 What this software does

The program analyzes each frame of your video. It adds detail that was not there before. You can use it to upscale old home movies or clean up digital recordings. It makes images look crisp on modern screens.

⚙️ System Requirements

  • Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64-bit).
  • Intel or AMD processor with at least 2 GHz speed.
  • 8 GB of system memory.
  • Dedicated graphics card with at least 4 GB of video memory.
  • 10 GB of free storage space.

🖥️ How to use the program

Once you finish the install, you can launch the program from the icon on your desktop.

  • Load Video: Click the open button to find the file you want to change.
  • Choose Model: Pick a model based on your video type. Use the upscale model for low-resolution files. Use the denoise model for grainy clips.
  • Set Output: Choose where you want to save your new file.
  • Start Process: Click the start button. The software shows you a progress bar while it works. Processing time depends on your computer hardware and file length.

📌 Frequently Asked Questions

Does it work on older Windows versions? This version requires Windows 10 or newer.

Can I stop the process? Yes, you can click the cancel button. The file will save up to the frame where you stopped.

Why is the process slow? Enhancing high-definition video takes significant effort from your computer processor and graphics card. This normal behavior ensures high quality.
